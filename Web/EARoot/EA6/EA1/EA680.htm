<p>Closely connected to the termination rules are the outcome rules. When a dialogue terminates, the outcome rules determine its success or otherwise. In many cases this is based on the content of commitment stores (e.g. a dialogue might be successful if a certain proposition is in all participants' commitment stores). As with the termination rules, the outcome rules will vary depending on the aim of the dialogue<br/></p>
